Our Story

Food is a big part of a city's cultural life, and Pizza Mark has become an essential part of the Los Angeles community. Since partnering with Slice in 2020, we've been serving up the best kosher pizza on Robertson Boulevard.

Los Angeles residents have made Pizza Mark one of the most popular pizzerias in the area. From our famous cheese pizza to our fresh falafel, every dish is made with premium ingredients and a commitment to the highest kosher standards.

We proudly maintain Cholov Yisroel and Pas Yisroel certification — so you can enjoy every bite with confidence.
